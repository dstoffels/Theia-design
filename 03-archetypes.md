## Archetypes

### Domains
Every skill belongs to a group of related skills called a ***Domain*** that it collectively contributes SP to as it levels up, along with the other skills in the domain. A domain is also the base ***archetype*** for the character, which levels up at 100SP, unlocking at level 1. Once unlocked, you can choose to elect a domain, the first step in defining your character's archetype, which unlocks archetypal skill trees with unique abilities and bonuses.

There are 4 primary domains: Warrior, Rogue, Mystic, and Mage. There are also 3 professional domains: Builder, Settler, and Scholar. Domains and professions are only mutually exclusive to each other, meaning you can choose one primary domain and one profession.

| Domain | Skills | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Warrior | Bludgeons, Polearms, Swords, Defense | Warriors specialize in melee combat, utilizing infantry weapons, shields and armor. |
| Rogue | Shortblades, Archery, Unarmed, Subterfuge | Rogues employ unconventional combat, relying on ranged weapons, stealth and precision. |
| Mystic | Animism, Manifestation, Psionism, Speechcraft | Mystics embrace the mystical arts, harnessing the power of the mind and spirit. |
| Mage | Cohesion, Entropics, Galvanics, Displacement | Mages manipulate the fundamental forces of nature, wielding elemental magic. |

| Profession | Skills | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Builder | Woodworking, Masonry, Metallurgy | Builders are skilled in crafting, construction and engineering with wood, stone and metal. |
| Settler | Survival, Husbandry, Outfitting | Settlers are the backbone of civilization, from basic survival to agriculture and tailoring. |
| Scholar | Alchemy, Artifacts, Spellbinding | Scholars are dedicated to the pursuit of knowledge, mastering various academic and magical disciplines. |

### Subdomains
Your character can further be refined by tier 2 archetypes, which are a subset of a primary domain. There are two paths you can take with tier 2 archetypes, ***Subdomains*** and ***Specializations***, which are mutually exclusive to each other. A subdomain combines two primary domains into a single archetype, unlocking the skill trees of each and a unique skill tree for that archetype. A subdomain unlocks once both of its parent domains are unlocked and one of the two are already elected.

| Subdomain | Domains | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Skirmisher | Warrior + Rogue | Skirmishers are ruthless and cunning fighters, with wide ranging knowledge of weapons and tactics. |
| Cleric | Warrior + Mystic | Clerics combine martial prowess and the mystic arts. |
| Battlemage | Warrior + Mage | Battlemages are versatile combatants, adept at both martial and magical combat. |
| Ranger | Rogue + Mage | Rangers use elemental magic to enhance their cunning abilities. |
| Monk | Rogue + Mystic | Monks are agile and disciplined combatants, blending cunning with spiritual focus. |
| Sorcerer | Mystic + Mage | Sorcerers have wide ranging magical knowledge, studying both mystic arts and elemental forces. |

### Specialization

Each skill is also associated with a single tier 2 archetype called a ***Specialization***, allowing you to specialize in a single skill. A specialization unlocks unique abilities further along the skill tree that are only available when specializing in that skill. Specializations unlock with two prerequisites: skill level 300 and domain level 20, and are mutually exclusive with all other tier 2 archetypes.

Finally, the tier 3 archetype is a secondary, late-game specialization of a skill called a ***Subspec***, which unlocks at skill level 600 and domain level 40. A subspec can be elected in addition to any tier 2 archetype, but must reside in the character's elected domain(s).
