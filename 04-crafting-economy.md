# Crafting


## Materials

### Resources
Resources are nodes found in the wild where raw materials can be harvested.

#### Deposits
Deposits are finite resources of raw **minerals** that can be mined with viables tools, that directly correlate to the mineral they yield.

#### Trees
Trees can be chopped for wood and harvested for their seeds, sap and other byproducts. Cultivated trees in groves provide a constant source of these materials, while wild trees are finite resources.

**Skills**: Woodworking, Survival

| Tree | Action | Yield | Description |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Tree | Chop | Wood, Trunk, Sap | Large, generic trees of varying species |
| Yew Tree | Chop | Yew | A flexible conifer used for making bows |
| Bamboo Clump | Chop | Bamboo | Tall stalks used for making bows & blowguns |
| Mulberry Tree | Harvest | Silk Filaments, Mulberries | A source of silk used for making fine textiles |

#### Plants
Plants are a renewable resource that yield seeds, fibers, food and alchemical materials. Wild sources have smaller yields, but the seeds can be used in gardens and plantations as a constant, high-yield source of the plant's yields. Plants that yield berries do not yield seeds and vice versa, berries can be used as seeds.

**Skills**: Survival

### Refined Materials
Refined materials have been processed at a specialized station, often requiring multiple raw materials and minimum skill proficiency.

- Stations listed are the most basic station required to refine the material. Many stations are replaced by more advanced ones where the material can also be refined (campfire > hearth > fireplace).

#### Metal Ingots
Metals are recyclable materials that can be smelted repeatedly to yield ingots of a specific mass (1 ore = 1kg metal). Even metal items can be smelted back into ingots for repurposing. Leftover mass will be returned as a smaller ingot when crafting and multiple ingots can be combined in the smelting process for larger ingots.

### Components
Components are the building blocks for crafting items. Some are replaceable modules used to enhance existing items, like the pommel of a sword. Even after crafting, modules can be reclaimed by disassembling an item, allowing the player to repurpose them into entirely different items.

The materials used to make a component will impart their qualities into the crafted item. The durability of iron is greater than that of copper, but less than that of steel. For weapons, different materials affect weight or damage; for armor, its hindrance or damage reduction.

#### Blades
- Thin: A narrow blade with a sharp point.
- Broad: A wide, straight blade for crafting swords.
- Curved: A short, curved blade for crafting swords.
- Convex: A wide, curved blade for crafting swords.
- Leaf: A broad, rounded, 2-sided blade for crafting shortblades.
- Tapered: A triangular 2-sided blade for crafting daggers.
- Knife: A small, 1-sided blade for crafting knives.

## Equipment
Equipment items are crafted with a recipe of components and supplies. Some recipes are discovered through experimentation. Equipment can be disassembled to reclaim the components used to make them, allowing you to repurpose them into entirely different items.
